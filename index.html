BLACKSITE OPS was built by operators, for operators. Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate MilSim enthusiasts, we set out to create Bulgaria's most immersive and professional tactical gaming platform.

From elite competitive events to beginner-friendly skirmishes, every experience is designed with realism, safety, and camaraderie in mind.
